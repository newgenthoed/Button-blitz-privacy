Privacy Policy for Button Blitz

Last Updated: January 12, 2025

Overview

Button Blitz ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile game application.

Quick Summary

Button Blitz does not collect any personal information. Your game data stays only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Information Collection and Use

Data We DO NOT Collect

Button Blitz does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Social media information
  • Payment information
  • Photos or media files
  • Contact lists
  • Any other personal data

Data Stored Locally Only

Button Blitz stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • High scores
  • Game statistics (total hits, accuracy)
  • Achievement progress
  • Difficulty level preferences

Important: This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is not backed up to any cloud services
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Button Blitz does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Payment processors

Children's Privacy

Button Blitz is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from children or adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Security

Since Button Blitz does not collect or transmit any data, there is no data security risk. All game information is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security measures.

Data Retention

Local game data (scores, achievements) is retained on your device until:

  • You manually reset the game data
  • You clear the app's data/cache
  • You uninstall the app

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to:

  • Access
  • Modify
  • Delete
  • Export
  • Restrict processing of

Your game data is entirely under your control on your device.
